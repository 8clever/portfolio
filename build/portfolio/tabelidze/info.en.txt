
### Tabelidze

https://tabelidze.skilap.org

The project is implemented under the brand PushOk

The project aims to manage the time of employees. Off project.

Before the start of the day, you need to indicate what projects you will be involved in and the time you can afford to spend.
After you can start the day.
During the working day, you must indicate what project you are working on by clicking the Play button.
So during the day statistics are accumulated, where you can see how much you planned and how much time you actually spent.
After you can finish the day.

Cross-platform (desktop, mobile, web).

Technology Stack: Nodejs, React, Couchdb, Pouchdb, Electron, Phonegap

Team: 1 Full-stack developer

Position: Full-stack developer
